FAQ

Got questions?
We've got answers.

How quickly will I receive housing alerts?
+
Pro subscribers receive real-time alerts — typically within 1–2 hours of a new listing being posted that matches your criteria. Essential plan subscribers receive a daily morning digest. Speed matters in tight housing markets, which is why our Pro plan is designed for students who need to act fast.
How do you verify listings and filter scams?
+
Our system cross-references every listing against multiple databases: known scam landlord profiles, stolen photography databases, price anomaly detection, and registration checks where applicable. Any listing requesting advance payment via wire transfer, gift cards, or outside-platform methods is automatically flagged and excluded. We also manually review flagged items daily.
Which cities are covered?
+
We currently cover 119 cities across Europe, North America, and selected Asia-Pacific cities. This includes all major university cities: Amsterdam, Berlin, London, Paris, Brussels, Copenhagen, Vienna, Barcelona, Toronto, Melbourne, Tokyo and more. You can check full city coverage on our Cities page before subscribing.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
+
Yes, absolutely. Cancel anytime from your dashboard with one click — no questions asked, no cancellation fees. Your subscription remains active until the end of your billing period. We're confident you'll find housing before you need to cancel.
Do you offer refunds?
+
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on your first subscription month if you're not satisfied. After the first month, we don't offer prorated refunds, but you can always cancel before your next billing date to avoid future charges. Contact support@socials.community for any billing queries.
Will I receive too many alerts?
+
No. We prioritise quality over quantity. You set your criteria — budget range, room type, move-in date, preferred neighbourhoods — and we only send listings that match. Most Pro subscribers receive between 3–8 highly relevant alerts per day, not hundreds of generic notifications.
